Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026
This application is designed with user privacy as a priority. It does not require users to provide personally identifiable information such as real name, email address, phone number, or government-issued identification.

User Accounts
The application uses a simplified account system. No email, phone number, real name, or profile photo is required. User profiles may use emoji-based avatars only. Each user is identified by a unique internal identifier generated by the system.

Permissions
The application may request access to the following device features solely to enable core functionality:

• Camera – to allow users to capture photos and videos for publication within the app.
• Photo Library – to allow users to select and upload existing photos or videos.
• Location (Geolocation) – to optionally attach location information to user-generated content or enable location-related features.

Access to these features is requested only when necessary and is not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Stored Content
The application stores user-generated content such as messages, photos, videos, comments, likes, and related metadata. This data is stored on servers exclusively to ensure proper operation of the app’s features.

Purpose of Data Usage
Stored data is used strictly to provide application functionality, including content publishing, messaging, and user interaction. The data is not analyzed for marketing, advertising, profiling, or behavioral tracking.

No Advertising or Tracking
The application does not display advertisements and does not use third-party analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising SDKs. User data is not shared with third parties.

Data Security
Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

User Control
Users can manage and delete their own content within the application. Deleted content is removed from active systems in accordance with standard operational procedures.

Children’s Privacy
The application is not intended for children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Policy Updates
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the developer through the official application support channel.
